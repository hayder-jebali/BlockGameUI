Follow these steps to rejoin WeCoin blockchain !

First Step Download an Dapp app / browser or install Metamask

In order to navigate on the web site, you need to have a D-APP browser.
You can download the trust application available for IOS and android :

Second Step Create your blockchain account

Launch the application and follow the wizard to create your blockchain account. This account will be created on the ethereum blockchain. If you are on desktop, create it on your browser (via metamask or directly on your browser).

An account is identified by an hexadecimal number, like this one : 0x872B14603DC867A9e97211DEb6f82fE1Fa1788E4
You will give it to people who want to send you money.
Quite hard to remember, no ?
That's why we use QR code on blockchain :)

Third Step Put your app on Ropsten Blockchain

If you are using trust application, go to settings and choose Network:


Then choose the Ropsten network and accept the dialog box:

 Fourth Step You can now access your account !

You can now send and receive wecoins ! Go to the website with the dapp browser and you will see your wecoin account !
